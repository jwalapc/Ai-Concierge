## 02 — The 7 AI Moats You MUST Build

1. **Proprietary Hyper-Local Data** — User interactions → unique data on local preferences, merchant quality, timing, outcomes. Impossible for competitors to replicate.
2. **Workflow Embedding** — Own the full journey (discovery → decision → execution → follow-up). Switching costs and 30-50% higher retention.
3. **Network Effects (Data Flywheel)** — More users → better recommendations → more users. Compounding advantage.
4. **Hyper-Local Merchant Relationships** — Exclusive partnerships = supply-side lock-in and geography control.
5. **Vertical Specialization** — One vertical first (food, wellness, entertainment, home services). Deep domain beats general-purpose AI.
6. **Human-in-the-Loop Trust Layer** — Expert review for high-stakes decisions builds trust and defensibility.
7. **Community & Brand** — Power users who contribute feedback, train the AI, and advocate.

### Critical Mistakes to Avoid

- Being a "ChatGPT wrapper" (prompt + UI only)
- Trying to do everything — pick one vertical, dominate, then expand
- Ignoring unit economics from Day 1 (CAC, LTV, margins)
- Underestimating data moats (defensibility is data + workflows, not the model)
- Skipping regulatory compliance for healthcare, finance, or legal

## 04 — Specific Strategy Recommendations

### 1. Pick Your Vertical CAREFULLY

**Do:** Choose ONE of:
- Food discovery (personalized recommendations + reservations, not delivery)
- Wellness (fitness, nutrition, mental health)
- Entertainment (events, movies, experiences)
- Home services (repairs, cleaning, renovation)

Test 3 verticals; double down on best engagement.

### 2. Dominate One Geography First

Start with Bengaluru (or Mumbai/Delhi). Deep local merchant relationships + hyper-local data = network effects before expanding.

### 3. Prove AI Differentiation

Answer: "Why not ChatGPT?"
- Proprietary local data (merchant quality, timing, prices)
- End-to-end execution (not just recommendations)
- Learning from user outcomes (not just inputs)
- Integration with local services (bookings, payments, tracking)

### 4. Build the Feedback Loop

Track **outcomes**, not just decisions. Did the recommendation work? Outcome data = moat ChatGPT can't access.

### 5. Multiple Revenue Streams

- **Consumer:** Subscription ($10-20/mo) for premium
- **Merchant:** Commission on bookings/transactions
- **B2B:** White-label for credit cards, banks (e.g. Amex Concierge)

### 6. Speed is Your Early Moat

Move fast, iterate on feedback, build before competitors wake up to your vertical.
